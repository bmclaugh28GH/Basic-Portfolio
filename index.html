About Me

Hello! I've got over 30 years in software development, having supported applications supporting diverse goals such as personnel distribution and assignment, magazine circulation fulfillment for a business lobby, help desk issues tracking and administration, sales for hierarchy and commission tracking, and most recently, cable media advertising sales.

I'm originally from south Jersey (Go Eagles! Go Phillies!). I got a BA in computer science from Rutgers University, and served in the US Navy on a ship in Rhode Island and doing IT work in enlisted personnel assignment in northern Virginia. For the last twenty years I've supported domestic advertising sales for Discovery Communications.

I'm the proud father of two great kids; my wife and I live with them in Silver Spring, MD with our dog, Mabel. In my free time I enjoy playing guitar (poorly), reading, watching TV, and sneaking in a few minutes on my son's Xbox.
